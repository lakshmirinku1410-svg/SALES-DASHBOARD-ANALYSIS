# Sales Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Sales Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, slicers, KPIs, and Excel formulas.

The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify sales trends, evaluate product and regional performance, and make data-driven decisions through an interactive interface.

### Key Features

* Interactive dashboard with slicers for dynamic filtering
* KPI cards displaying:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Total Orders
  * Total Quantity Sold
  * Average Sales
  * Profit Margin
* Monthly and yearly sales trend analysis
* Category-wise and sub-category-wise sales analysis
* Region-wise sales and profit comparison
* Top-performing and low-performing products
* Customer segment analysis
* Dynamic charts that update automatically based on slicer selections

### Excel Features Used

* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* Conditional Formatting
* Excel Tables
* IF, SUMIF, SUMIFS, COUNTIF, COUNTIFS
* XLOOKUP
* INDEX-MATCH (where applicable)
* KPI Cards using Shapes
* Data Validation

### Business Insights

The dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which products contribute the most revenue and profit?
* Which months have the highest sales?
* Which customer segment is the most profitable?
* How do sales and profits vary across categories?
* What are the Year-over-Year (YoY) and Month-over-Month (MoM) growth trends?
* Which products require business attention due to low profitability?

### Outcome

The dashboard provides a centralized view of business performance, allowing users to monitor KPIs, compare trends, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making through an intuitive and interactive Excel interface.
